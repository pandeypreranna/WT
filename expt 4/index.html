About EcoMart 🌍

EcoMart is an eco-friendly e-commerce platform 🛒 designed to promote sustainable living through responsible shopping. This website is built using HTML and CSS and focuses on products that are reusable, biodegradable, and environmentally safe ♻️.

Our aim is to reduce plastic waste 🚫 and encourage green alternatives 🌿 for everyday needs. By choosing eco-friendly products, users contribute to a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable future 💚.
